FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you start.

PSXL supports direct CSV import from AKD Securities, JS Global, Topline Securities, Arif Habib, and most other PSX brokers. For unsupported formats, you can use the standard PSXL CSV template.
PSXL uses the Weighted Average Cost (WAC) method as the default, which is the standard adopted for PSX equities. FIFO is also available in Pro and Firm plans for comparison purposes.
PSXL computes capital gains tax and withholding tax based on FBR rates as currently published. However, you should always confirm figures with a qualified tax consultant before filing. PSXL is a ledger tool, not a tax advisory service.
